About Newston

We're the official student-led newspaper of Newton College, made up of passionate writers and creatives from Form II to Form V. What brings us together is a shared love for storytelling, journalism, and the impact words can have.

Our goal is simple: to connect our school community, to each other and to the world beyond our walls. Whether we're covering campus events, sharing fresh takes on global issues, or diving into pop culture, Newston is here to keep you informed, inspired, and part of the conversation.
